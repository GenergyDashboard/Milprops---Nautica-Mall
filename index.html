🌱 Understanding Environmental Impact

How we calculate the environmental benefits of solar generation
🌳Trees Equivalent

The number of mature trees needed to absorb the same amount of CO₂ avoided through solar generation. Based on trees absorbing approximately 22 kg of CO₂ per tree per year.

Trees = CO₂ Avoided / 22 kg per tree per year
🏠Homes Powered

How many homes could be powered with this clean energy, based on typical consumption of 9.8 kWh per household daily.

Households powered = Energy (kWh) / 9.8 kWh
⛏️kg Coal Saved

The amount of coal that would need to be burned to generate the same electricity we've produced with solar.

Coal Saved = Energy (kWh) × 0.548 kg coal per kWh
💧Litres Water Saved

Water saved by using solar instead of conventional power plants, which require significant water for cooling.

Water Saved = Energy (kWh) × 1 L water per kWh
